Legacy & Vision

Founded by Plovdiv Legend Geno Dobrevski

Our Roots

FC Veld-Plovdiv is more than just a football club; it is a center for excellence located at the heart of Plovdiv’s sports life—the Rowing Base. Established with a vision to build the next generation of Bulgarian football talent, the club combines professional standards with a passion for youth development.

The Founder: Geno Dobrevski

Geno Dobrevski, a legendary figure from Botev Plovdiv, founded Arena Veld to give back to the city. His experience at the highest level of football ensures that the training methodology at FC Veld is second to none.

Key Milestones

September 23, 2016

Grand Opening: Arena Veld officially opens with a massive youth tournament, introducing Plovdiv to a state-of-the-art football facility.

The Arena Today

7,000 sq.m. of Excellence: Our facility features specialized pitches for F5, F7, and F9 football, providing the perfect environment for all age groups.

Youth Championships

Competitive Spirit: FC Veld consistently participates in BFU (Bulgarian Football Union) youth championships, with strong teams in the U-14 and younger categories.

Located near the Youth Center at the Rowing Base, we continue to be Plovdiv’s premier destination for youth football.
